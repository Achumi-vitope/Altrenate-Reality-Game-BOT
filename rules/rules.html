RULES
  1. A player can initiate to form an alliance with one or more participants to complete a challenge.
    But remember, there can only be one winner.

  2. Top 3 players who completes each challenges first shall be given the authority to eliminate
    or kick out a player(may be more depending on the number of participants) after each challenge.

  3. If a 'top 3' player does not want any player(s) kicked, his wish shall be granted.
  4. Even if a participant fails to find the KEY of a challenge, he/she shall
    not be eliminated unless decided by the top 3 participants of a challenge.

  5. Every challenge will be in the form of either a Riddle, Puzzle, or a Mystery thing.
    The player must solve each Riddle/Question/Puzzle to obtain the KEY.

  6. Inorder to get to the top 3 in a challenge, you must find the KEY and login into the DOOR using your USERNAME, ID, and the KEY.

  7. There may or may not be a hint. It will depend on my mood
